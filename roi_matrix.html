Phase 1 — Identify: where are you losing the most value?

Pick your industry to preload realistic workflows, score each one on five dimensions, and watch the priority board rank them live. The best AI starting point is the workflow that is high-pain, high-repetition, high-impact, and AI-feasible all at once.

The better question is not "Where can we use AI?" — it is "Where are we losing the most value?"

Value loss in every business shows up in six areas. The workflows that leak the most value in these six areas are exactly the workflows where AI pays back fastest.

Phase 2 — Quantify: what does it save in dollars?

Pick the workflow you want to quantify. Enter a few inputs — we compute monthly and annual labor savings, and optionally error-cost savings. Add a volume number to reframe the math as cost per transaction, which is what your CFO actually cares about.
